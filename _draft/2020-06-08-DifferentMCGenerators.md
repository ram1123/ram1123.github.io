*** Discussion title: SMP: Multiboson

Dear all,

Please take note of the following announcement of a new public Monte
Carlo simulation. This is of particular interest for diboson analyses
making measurements sensitive to resummation (such as low values of
pT(VV)).

Best,

Kenneth

Dear colleagues,

the MATRIX+RadISH code is now publicly available for download on

https://matrix.hepforge.org/matrix+radish.html

The interface was introduced here: https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.07720 with
a comprehensive study for WW production.

This code allows you to resum various observables for all colour singlet
processes available in MATRIX (including Higgs, DY, Diboson, ...), while
being able to apply arbitrary fiducial cuts:

- transverse-momentum resummation of the system of colour singlet particles
  at NNLO+N3LL

- jet-veto resummation (and resummation of the leading jet pT spectrum)
  at NNLO+NNLL.

- joint resummation of both observables, allowing you for instance to
  obtain the transverse momentum spectrum of the colour singlet with
  a jet veto

For any questions don't hesitate to contact as and please circulate the
information among you colleagues.

Best wishes, Marius Wiesemann
